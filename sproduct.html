Tagliatelle Spinach

4KM 300gr

Product Details

These delicious tagliatelle nests combine hearty durum wheat semolina and flavourful green spinach to create a delicious pasta speciality in a striking green colour. Thanks to the roughened surface, sauces stick much better to the pasta. It is also an excellent side dish for fish, meat and vegetable dishes.

Ingredients: spinach, eggs, flour, salt
